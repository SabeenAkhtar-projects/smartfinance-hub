💡 Smart Budget Tips
Personalised advice based on your numbers
🛒
Plan Your Groceries
A weekly grocery list reduces spending by 20–25%. Buy in bulk for staples like rice, lentils, and oil.
📱
Audit Subscriptions
Cancel any subscription you haven't used in 30 days. That PKR 800/month = PKR 9,600 saved per year.
⚡
Reduce Utility Bills
Turn off ACs when leaving rooms. Use energy-saver bulbs. Small habits save PKR 2,000–4,000 monthly.
🏦
Automate Savings
Set up an auto-transfer on payday. What leaves your account first is what you actually save.
